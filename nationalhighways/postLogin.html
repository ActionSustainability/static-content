Our social value tool

We have developed a tool which allows us to collate, quantify and evidence the social value outcomes of our investment in England’s Strategic Road Network – in real-time and at project level.

This cloud-based reporting system has been specifically designed to enable National Highways supplier partners (including main contractors, sub-contractors and materials suppliers) to report against our specified social value metrics.

Reporting dashboards will allow our major frameworks and work programmes to bring together and aggregate social value data from a range of companies involved in the supply chain.

At a corporate level, National Highways will be able to analyse and communicate collective social value outcomes to our stakeholders, and how these contribute to our overall business goals.


Benefits of using the tool

Engaging with us and reporting your data will not only help National Highways, it will help your organisation (as a valued supplier partner) to:

  • Identify your own organisation’s social value contribution
  • Meet client expectations to report your social value impacts, using a simple and standard approach
  • Collect evidence to inform your tender responses on social value generated by your organisation
  • Focus attention within your organisation on Social Value and align your approach to a major infrastructure partner
