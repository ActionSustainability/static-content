SCS are working together to deliver HS2 along the 26.4km of HS2’s journey to its southern terminus in Euston, 21km of which will be in tunnels. The JV will complete challenging works taking the railway from West Ruislip to Euston via Old Oak Common, building a variety of structures requiring expert precision and care.

SCS is a founding member of Empowering Communities; the first programme of its kind to be led by the construction industry to deliver long-lasting benefits for local people for generations to come. The programme aims to create jobs for those experiencing worklessness and homelessness, help young people develop skills and access apprenticeships, and support community projects.

SCS are a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School

SCS aims to leave a positive legacy on the HS2 project, for society and our industry. We will thrive together through collaboration with our supply chain, empowering our diverse people to be operational excellent to ensure our strategic objectives and goals are realised.

SCS recognises the importance of embedding the principles of sustainability throughout our supply chain and the importance of our supply chain partners in achieving our sustainability goals and objectives.

These objectives include:

  • Carbon Reduction
  • Social value
  • Modern Slavery
  • Skills, employment and education
  • Improving the industries understanding of sustainability and sustainability practices
