Sizewell C: Our sustainability ambition

Sizewell C’s vision is “The Power of Good for Britain”. The project has five Strategic Aims that underpin this vision. We have developed five Supply Chain Goals that identify where our supply chain can support our sustainability.







Your role in our sustainability journey

Supply chain requirements have been developed, and placed within each contract, to ensure SZC meets its statutory obligations as well as strive towards industry leadership. These requirements therefore include the need to report performance data for the Supply Chain Goals and more. The reports you submit through this system are essential for:

  • Meeting the contractual obligations under your contract
  • Measuring progress towards SZC commitments to the local community and the wider UK
  • Meeting the project’s obligations to Local Authorities, Government, Shareholders and more
  • Enabling the project to monitor, evaluate and improve its Environmental, Social, Governance “ESG” performance throughout construction


Getting started with reporting

To facilitate a seamless reporting experience, we’ve prepared:

  • Comprehensive training: for an in-depth understanding, view our training sessions and download the detailed guides tailored for both reporters and approvers.
