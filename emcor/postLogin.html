EMCOR UK is a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School

As part of our commitment to supporting a sustainable supply chain, we are a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School.

We ask all our supply chain partners to become active members of the School, participating in the School’s learning and development programme, which is free to join.

We work closely with our supply chain parners, and those of our customers, on responsible and sustainable sourcing, and are committed to improving the competency, efficiency and diversity within our supply chain. We were the first facilities management provider to be assessed against ISO 20400 in sustainable procurement. This provides our customers with a proven, and independently verified, sustainable procurement methodology that delivers social and environmental benefits as well as cost savings.

We ask our supply chain partners to sign up to our Sustainability Pledge to demonstrate their commitment in providing and reporting data for the Sustainability Tool and by becoming active members in the Supply Chain Sustainability School
