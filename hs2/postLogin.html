HS2 and the Sustainability Tool

HS2 are working with The Sustainability Tool to report, measure and manage the performance of their full supply chain. In collaboration with EKFB, SCS, BBV and Align JV, the Sustainability Tool allows suppliers and subcontractors with be able to report their work on HS2 against a range of social, environmental and economic sustainability issues, aligning with their PMU reporting requirements.


HS2 as a Supply Chain Sustainability School


The Supply Chain School is working closely with HS2, its Main Works Civil Contractors and Station Contractors to offer a combined, strategic learning and development programme for all current and prospective suppliers and sub-contractors.

The HS2 Supply Chain School initiative uses a collaborative approach to learning and competence linked to HS2 strategic objectives. It also rationalises the approaches used to manage and monitor complex supply chain sustainability performance.
