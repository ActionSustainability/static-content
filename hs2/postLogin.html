HS2 and the Sustainability Tool

HS2 are working with The Sustainability Tool to report, measure and manage the performance of their full supply chain. In collaboration with EKFB, SCS, BBV and Align JV, the Sustainability Tool allows suppliers and subcontractors with be able to report their work on HS2 against a range of social, environmental and economic sustainability issues, aligning with their PMU reporting requirements.


HS2 as a Supply Chain Sustainability School


The Supply Chain School is working closely with HS2, its Main Works Civil Contractors and Station Contractors to offer a combined, strategic learning and development programme for all current and prospective suppliers and sub-contractors.

The HS2 Supply Chain School initiative uses a collaborative approach to learning and competence linked to HS2 strategic objectives. It also rationalises the approaches used to manage and monitor complex supply chain sustainability performance.



Our Commitments

We are committed to improving lives, communities and the engineering and construction industry. We will do this by providing sustained employment opportunities, protecting the environment and respecting people and places.

EKFB is a joint venture that brings together international, market leading expertise from four leading civil engineering and construction companies: Eiffage, Kier, Ferrovial Construction and BAM Nuttall.

EKFB is proud to have been appointed by HS2 to deliver civil engineering works across an 80km section of the new high speed rail link.

EKFB are a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School

EKFB recognise the importance of an integrated and collaborative supply chain which is essential for realising our sustainability and environmental objectives and aspirations. Utilising the sustainability school resources enables EKFB to engage and work with our supply chain partners, bringing confidence and assurance that we are utilising with an environmentally and ethically responsible supply chain.

The School enables EKFB to work alongside our supply chain and stakeholders to continually improve our knowledge and awareness of environmental and sustainable issues, leaving a lasting legacy on the projects we work, where we have uplifted knowledge and performance of the wider construction industry.

Waste
