Sustainability and the Supply Chain

Keeping People Warm, while Protecting the Plant, is at the heart of what Cadent does. We are committed to tackling climate change and supporting the transition to a sustainable energy system to keep the energy flowing for future generations.

To achieve our sustainability ambitions, we need to bring our people and partners on the journey with us. We are working with our supply chain to drive improved performance, reduce emissions, contribute to our sustainability goals and continue to meet our regulatory commitments.

Cadent are a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School

We are a member of the Supply Chain Sustainability School to help embed sustainability in our business and across our supply chain. Being a partner of the School will enable us to broaden our understanding (including that of our suppliers) of the impact we have on the environment. With the School’s help and the platform provided, we can build on our knowledge to meet our sustainability goals and continue our journey to net-zero emissions.

With safety, sustainability, and responsibility at the heart of all we do, we are using the Action Sustainability carbon reporting tool to strengthen sustainability performance and reporting with our supply chain partners.

Suppliers should report on materials, fuel use and other environmental metrics associated with activities undertaken on behalf of Cadent by completing the Cadent carbon tool on a monthly basis, with an option of submitting quarterly for manufacturers. This helps both our suppliers and Cadent monitor and measure the carbon footprint.

For further Cadent environmental reporting guidance, please contact the Environment Team at box.environmental2@cadentgas.com.

Cadent’s Environmental Goals

Cadent’s Environmental Action Plan for RIIO 3 (2026-2031) sets ambitious goals across carbon reduction, resource efficiency, biodiversity, and operational impact. Contractors and suppliers play a critical role in almost every commitment area.

1. Climate and carbon commitments:

a. Delivering Cadent’s Mains replacement programme
b. Reporting embodied carbon for materials and construction activities
c. Providing emission data across your activities
d. Reducing emissions from operations and projects

2. Sustainable Use of Resources

a. Reducing excavated spoil to landfill
b. Using recycled aggregate in backfill/reinstatement materials
c. Helping reduce PE pipe waste and the use of single-use plastics
d. Embedding circular economy principles
