Sustainability and the Supply Chain

To achieve our sustainability ambitions, we need to bring our people and partners on the journey with us. We are working with our supply chain to drive improved performance, contribute to our sustainability goals and continue to meet our regulatory commitments.

Cadent are a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School

We have become a member of the Supply Chain Sustainability School to further embed sustainability in our business and across our supply chain. Being a partner of the School will enable us to broaden our understanding (including that of our suppliers) of the impact we have on the environment. With the School’s help and the platform provided, we can build on our knowledge to meet our sustainability goals and continue our journey to net zero emissions.

We bring gas to 11 million homes and businesses throughout the North West, West Midlands, East Midlands, South Yorkshire, East of England and North London. We're committed to keeping you safe and warm.

We are committed to tackling climate change and supporting the transition to a sustainable energy system to keep the energy flowing for future generations.

With safety, sustainability, and responsibility at the heart of all we do, we are using the Action Sustainability carbon reporting tool to strengthen sustainability performance and reporting with our supply chain partners.

Suppliers must report on the carbon associated with activities undertaken on behalf of Cadent by completing the Cadent carbon tool on a monthly basis, with an option of submitting quarterly for manufacturers.

Cadent goals

Our Environmental Action Plan (EAP) explains how we will take responsibility for the environmental impacts of our network and business operations during RIIO-2 price control period. Through the delivery of actions here, we will drive environmental improvements, reduce our carbon footprint, and efficiently manage our recourse use.

EAP 8 – To measure and reduce the carbon intensity of PE pipe and fittings by the end of RIIO-2

EAP 9 – To work with our supply chain to measure and reduce scope 3 emissions for business carbon footprint reporting

EAP 12 – Less than 5% of our waste from excavations will be sent to landfill

EAP 13 – Less than 10% of our backfill will be first use aggregate in the North West and East of England. Less than 5% in the West Midlands and North London.
