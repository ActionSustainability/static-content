EKFB are a Partner of the Supply Chain Sustainability School

The school enables EKFB to work alongside our supply chain and stakeholders to continually improve our knowledge and awareness of environmental and sustainable issues, leaving a lasting legacy on the projects we work, where we have uplifted knowledge and performance of the wider construction industry.

HELPING SSE REACH​ THEIR 2030 GOALS​

Sustainability is one of SSE’s core values, defined as ‘we do things responsibly to add long-term value’. We’re committed to being operationally net zero by 2050 and have set four 2030 business goals aligned with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

SSE’s four business goals for 2030 put sustainability at the heart of our business. First set in 2019, the 2030 Goals were refreshed in February 2022 to increase ambition and drive faster decarbonisation.

SSE’s supply chain enables it to deliver its business strategy and create value. SSE therefore aims to work with suppliers and contractors to ensure its key values are supported, managed and, where possible, improved through stronger relationships, innovation and enhanced management of shared risks and opportunities.

SSE’s Sustainable Procurement Code outlines the expectations and requirements on SSE’s suppliers and contractors, and their supply chains, when undertaking work on SSE’s behalf. You can see the full Sustainable Procurement Code here and guidance for suppliers here.
