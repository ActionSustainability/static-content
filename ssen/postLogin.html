Who we are

SSE Renewables is a leading developer and operator of renewable energy, headquartered in the UK and Ireland, with a growing presence internationally. We have an operational portfolio of around 4GW of onshore wind, offshore wind and hydro generation, with a secured future project pipeline of over 13GW in development.

Our operational portfolio comprises nearly 2GW of onshore wind capacity, almost 0.5GW of offshore wind capacity, and almost 1.5GW of flexible hydro power and pumped storage capacity. These generation assets produce around 10TWh of renewable power each year. We're committed to delivering the green energy the world needs now and in the future. Our 13GW+ construction and development pipeline includes 3.5GW of onshore wind, 1.5GW of pumped storage, and 8.4GW of offshore wind energy - the largest offshore development pipeline in the UK and Ireland.

We know offshore wind will be the backbone of a net zero power system and that's why we're building more offshore wind than any other company in the world right now. This includes the world's largest offshore wind farm, the 3.6GW Dogger Bank project in the North Sea, and the world's deepest fixed-bottom offshore wind farm, the 1.1GW Seagreen project off the Scottish coast.

We're a team of around 1,500 renewable energy professionals based across the UK, Ireland, Spain, France, Italy, Greece, the Netherlands, Japan and the USA. We've honed our skills through over 15 years of delivering world-leading projects with expertise in project design and optimisation, consenting and stakeholder engagement, financing, procurement, construction, and operations.

Who we are

SSEN Transmission’s first priority is to provide a safe and reliable supply of electricity to the communities it serves. It does this by making sure its employees and contractors work safely while ensuring the network is able to function properly and meet electricity demand now and in the future.

Scotland’s transmission network has a strategic role to play in supporting delivery of the UK and Scotland’s Net Zero targets. We’re already a mass exporter of renewable energy, with around two thirds of power generated in our network area exported to demand centres further south. By 2050, the north of Scotland is expected to need 40GW of low carbon energy capacity to support net zero delivery. For context, we currently have around 8GW of renewable generation connected in the north of Scotland.

Sustainability

Our sustainability plan sets out our ambitions and targets to enable a smart, flexible, resilient, and sustainable distributed energy system. The plan has assessed our material risks and opportunities and aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and wider SSE Group Net Zero Action Plan commitments.

Our sustainability targets include:

  • Reduce the carbon intensity of scope 1 GHG emissions by 80% by 2030, from the 2017/18 baseline.
  • Reduce absolute scope 1 and 2 GHG emissions by 72.5% by 2030 from a 2017/18 base year.
  • Reduce absolute GHG emissions from the use of products sold by 50% by 2034 from a 2017/18 base year.
  • Zero controlled waste to landfill by 2026.
  • Achieve Biodiversity Net Gain on new large capital infrastructure structure projects.
  • We are committed to powering over 1.5 million miles/day of zero-emission transport, through our investment in up to 500 charging bays, powered by renewable energy across the UK, by 2030.
  • By 2035 we will collect and transport 250MW of renewable energy, through our Independent Networks providing low-carbon solutions to our clients.
  • Become a trusted regional community partner that challenges inequality and reduces environmental impact by delivering and reporting on our four commitments (community partner, inclusive recruitment, low carbon education, and environment) to key stakeholders by 2025.

Users

There are four distinct user roles designed to facilitate the collection of sustainability data from our supply chain partners.​

Administrator

This is a SSEN Transmission employee who is a Project Coordinator, or a Project Manager within Network and Connections.

System Administrator

This is a SSEN Transmission employee within a sustainability focused role.

Viewer

This is a SSEN Transmission employee who is a Project Manager; however, Environmental Managers may also gain access to this role.

Reporter

This is the supply chain contact who has been identified to provide and submit the applicable sustainability data.
