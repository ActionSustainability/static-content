THE SUSTAINABILITY DATA ​CAPTURE TOOL (SDCT)

As a cloud-based sustainability performance reporting system, specifically designed to manage supply-chain sustainability performance, SSEN Transmission contractors are invited to report on specified sustainability data.

For each project:

  • An embodied carbon estimate report for designs at the beginning of each project download template here
  • An annual 'as built' embodied carbon report [download template here]. This allows the performance of projects to be monitored as the SDCT converts this performance into carbon emissions.
  • A monthly environmental report [download question set here and the waste log here]
  • An annual social value report [download question set here]

For your organisation:

  • An annual company survey [download question set here]​

The sustainability data reported will be visible , in real-time, at a project level. Dashboards will communicate performance and will be utilised to measure targets and track progress.

At the business unit and corporate level, SSEN Transmission will review the performance of each organisation in the supply chain and as a collective. Targets are set by SSEN Transmission to encourage collaboration and achieve SSE’s business goals for 2030.​

HELPING SSE REACH​ THEIR 2030 GOALS​

Sustainability is one of SSE’s core values, defined as ‘we do things responsibly to add long-term value’. We’re committed to being operationally net zero by 2050 and have set four 2030 business goals aligned with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

SSE’s four business goals for 2030 put sustainability at the heart of our business. First set in 2019, the 2030 Goals were refreshed in February 2022 to increase ambition and drive faster decarbonisation.

SSE’s supply chain enables it to deliver its business strategy and create value. SSE therefore aims to work with suppliers and contractors to ensure its key values are supported, managed and, where possible, improved through stronger relationships, innovation and enhanced management of shared risks and opportunities.

SSE’s Sustainable Procurement Code outlines the expectations and requirements on SSE’s suppliers and contractors, and their supply chains, when undertaking work on SSE’s behalf. You can see the full Sustainable Procurement Code here and guidance for suppliers here.

Engaging and reporting data will not only help SSEN Transmission, it will help you to

  • Reduce costs through increased efficiency
  • Meet client expectations to report your sustainability impacts, once and in a simple way
  • Give better responses to bid questions on your organisation’s actions to reduce sustainability impacts
  • Identify priorities to be more efficient with your use of resources
