THE SUSTAINABILITY DATA ​CAPTURE TOOL (SDCT)

SSEN Transmission contractors are invited to report sustainability data by creating a free account. Once registered and logged in, the following is required:

For each project:

  • An embodied carbon estimate report at the beginning of each project [download template here]
  • An actual embodied carbon report at the end of each project [download template here]. This allows the performance of projects to be monitored as the SDCT converts this performance into carbon emissions.
  • A monthly environmental report [download question set here and the waste log here]
  • A monthly social value report [download question set here]

For your organisation:

  • An annual company survey [download question set here]​

The sustainability data reported will be visible , in real-time, at a project level. Dashboards will communicate performance and will be utilised to measure targets and track progress.

At the business unit and corporate level, SSEN Transmission will review the performance of each organisation in the supply chain and as a collective. Targets are set by SSEN Transmission to encourage collaboration and achieve SSE’s business goals for 2030.​

HELPING SSE REACH​ THEIR 2030 GOALS​

SSEN Transmission are working to deliver a network for net zero and is committed to operating in a just, sustainable way which creates benefit and wealth for the communities, stakeholders and customers we serve

SSEN has set four fundamental goals for 2030, directly linked to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, which puts sustainability at the heart of SSE’s business strategy. Their vision is to be ‘a leading energy provider in a net-zero world’.

The aim is to share the economic, social, and environmental opportunities created by SSE as they reach their 2030 Goals.
