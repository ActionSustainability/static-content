STRENGTHENING SUSTAINABILITY PERFORMANCE

SSE plc is a UK-listed energy company that is driven by the purpose to provide energy needed today while building a better world of energy for tomorrow; and its vision is to be a leading energy company in a net-zero world. Belonging to the SSE Group, Scottish Hydro Electric Transmission (SSEN Transmission), are responsible for the electricity transmission network in the north of Scotland. SSEN Transmission is utilising the Sustainability Data Capture Tool to strengthen sustainability performance within the supply chain.
