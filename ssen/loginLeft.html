STRENGTHENING SUSTAINABILITY ​PERFORMANCE

SSE plc is a UK-listed energy company that is driven by the purpose to provide energy needed today while building a better world of energy for tomorrow; and its vision is to be a leading energy company in a net-zero world.

Belonging to the SSE Group, SSEN Transmission are responsible for the electricity transmission network in the North of Scotland and Scottish islands and play a critical role in the transition to a low carbon future.

With safety, sustainability, and responsibility at the heart of all we do, SSEN Transmission are utilising the Sustainability Data Capture Tool to strengthen sustainability performance within our supply chain and to ensure a network for net zero.
