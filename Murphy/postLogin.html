Our Carbon Calculator

As part of our commitment to minimising our impact, we will be using this cloud-based ‘carbon calculator’ reporting tool, which allows our Murphy project teams to track their carbon emissions in real- time, against a calculated baseline.



Benefits of using the tool

This tool helps individual projects take ownership of their emissions and incorporate any client or internal targets. This focus will encourage carbon reduction and drive low carbon innovations and solutions. This will, in turn, mean that Murphy Group is able to collate data across the three geographies of our business and ensure we are aligned and on track with our Murphy at 75 target of being net zero by 2030.
